Budget breakdown

Full overview of the cost per participant

Transparancy matters.

The Velotour Festival is a not-for-profit event. The organising team only wants to make sure that all costs get covered among all participants. Therefore, we created this page to give you a good understanding where the 120 euro participation fee per person comes from.
