Budget breakdown

Full overview of the cost per participant

Transparancy matters.

The VeloTour Festival is a not-for-profit event. The organising team of volunteers only wants to make sure that all costs get covered among all participants. Therefore, we created this page to give you a good understanding where the 75 euro participation fee per person comes from.

Full cost overview per participant
Cost Description Price
2x dinner Dinner on Friday and Saturday night.

Logistics This includes cooking equipment, bike mechanic, material and rent toilets. €16,68
Fee Cactus volunteers Volunteers of Cactus will guide us from Brussel to Flanders on the first day. €4
VeloTour gadget A nice gadget to keep good memories of this first edition. €5
Insurance Liability and physical damage insurance for volunteers and a basic physical damage insurance for all participants. €3
Solidarity contribution With 4 extra euro, we support the participation of 6 people who don't have the financial means to participate. €4
Online documentation Volunteer remuneration for documenting the whole concept of the VeloTour Festival under an open license. We want to make it easy for everyone to organize a VeloTour Festival! €4
Use of Open Collective Costs associated with the use of the platform and the legal entity of Open Collective Brussels asbl. €4,50
Overhead 10% margin for unexpected costs, to make sure we can cover all costs. In case we don't need this money, we'll use it for next year's edition. €6,82
Total price Price per participant for 3 days of VeloTour Festival. €75
